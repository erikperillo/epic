CVPR 2018 policy on dual submission and arxiv appears at: http://cvpr2018.thecvf.com/submission/main_conference/author_guidelines. 1) Comparison datasets: Authors have compared with other models with only MIT300 dataset. The authors should the performance of proposed method in other challenging datasets such as PASCAL-S, DUT-OMRON, IS and CAT2000.

2) Loss function: The authors have proposed to use cross-correlation as their loss function as compared to euclidean or similarity loss by other works. But the intuition behind this is not clear. Also, as the terms used in equation from lines 366-368 are not explained, it is not possible for the reader to understand the idea of using cross-correlation loss function. It would be interesting to see how much improvement can one get by using cross-correlation loss function.

3) Evaluation metric: The authors have not compared with sAUC and KL metric. sAUC is important to identify the central bias of the model. So, the authors are requested to evaluate their model with respect to sAUC and KL metric.

4) The equations are not numbered. The explanation of the terms and notations used in equations are missing, thus confusing for the readers to assume the meaning of the equation (For e.g, equations in line 366-368).

5)In qualitative evaluation, the authors have shown only result by their method and ground truth. It would be better to show the results by other methods as well for better comparison.
